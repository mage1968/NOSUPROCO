Who We Are

The NOSUPROCO Brotherhood

The Negros Occidental Supreme Provincial Council (NOSUPROCO) is the governing body of Tau Gamma Phi — the Triskelion Grand Fraternity — across the province of Negros Occidental, Philippines.

Founded on October 4, 2008, NOSUPROCO stands as a unifying force for all Triskelion chapters and brothers within Negros Occidental. We uphold the pillars of brotherhood, honor, and service to our community and province.

As part of the Tau Gamma Phi Triskelions' Grand Fraternity/Sorority — established in 1968 with the latin phrase Fortis Voluntas Fraternitas — our council carries forward a legacy of strength, unity, and voluntary brotherhood that has endured for generations.

What We Stand For

Our Core Pillars

Brotherhood

A bond forged beyond the schools and barangay community — our brotherhood spans across all chapters in Negros Occidental, united by one oath, one heart, one name: Triskelion.

We push each brother toward academic, professional, and personal excellence — because a Triskelion who rises, lifts the entire brotherhood with him.

We celebrate and protect the rich cultural heritage of Negros Occidental — from MassKara to the haciendas — as proud Triskelions of this island we call home.

Our Story

Legacy & History

1968
Tau Gamma Phi Founded

Tau Gamma Phi, also known as the Triskelion Grand Fraternity, was founded on October 4, 1968, at the University of the Philippines Diliman in Quezon City. Driven by the core principles of Fortis Voluntas Fraternitas (Fortitude of Strength, Voluntarism/Freewill, and Brotherhood/Sisterhood), the fraternity was established by six founding fathers: The fraternity grew to become one of the largest and most respected fraternities in the Philippines, with sister sorority Tau Gamma Sigma (1969).

Pre-ERA from the 1980, 1990, and 2000s
Triskelions Take Root in Negros Occidental

Tau Gamma Phi chapters began establishing their presence across the cities and municipalities of Negros Occidental, spreading the values of brotherhood, honor, and service to the Negrense community.

October 4, 2008
NOSUPROCO Established

The Negros Occidental Supreme Provincial Council (NOSUPROCO) was formally established on October 4, 2008 — unifying all Community and School based chapters across the province under one governing body and amplifying the fraternity's impact in Western Visayas.

Present
NOSUPROCO Continues the Legacy

Today, NOSUPROCO stands strong — coordinating brotherhood activities, community service programs, and provincial assemblies across Negros Occidental. The Triskelion spirit burns brighter than ever in the sugar capital of the Philippines.
